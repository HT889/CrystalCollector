Crystal Collector

You are given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number that was generated at the start of the game. The value of each gem is hidden from you. All numbers are reset after each round.
